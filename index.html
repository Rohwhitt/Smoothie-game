How to Play

A recipe will display for 6-15 seconds depending on difficulty. The aim is to try memorise all the ingredients and add them to the smoothie.

Be careful though, as adding to much, too little, or the wrong ingredients will lose you points.

Lets see how good your memory is… oh and have fun!
